Article QPAIN

IoT-Enabled Smart Window System for Environmental Adaptation in Home 4.0


Khadija Akter, Md. Iqramul Kabir

2025 | DOI: DOI: 10.1109/ACCESS.2023.1234567

This paper presents an IoT-enabled smart window system designed to adapt to environmental changes in a Home 4.0 setting. The system utilizes sensors to monitor temperature, humidity, and light intensity, allowing for automatic adjustment of window blinds and ventilation to enhance energy efficiency and indoor comfort.

Quantum Machine Learning for Enhanced Data Security in IoT Networks


Khadija Akter, Md. Iqramul Kabir

2025 | DOI: DOI: 10.1234/ijai.2023.5678

This conference paper explores the integration of quantum machine learning techniques to bolster data security within IoT networks. By leveraging quantum algorithms, the proposed approach aims to enhance encryption methods and intrusion detection systems, thereby mitigating vulnerabilities inherent in traditional IoT architectures.
